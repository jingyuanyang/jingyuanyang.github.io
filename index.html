Jingyuan Yang

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Information Technology of the Department of Management Science and Information Systems at the Rutgers Business School, Rutgers, the State University of New Jersey, under the supervision of Professor Hui Xiong. My research focuses on data mining and business analytics with applications in marketing. You can download my CV here, and my LinkedIn Page here

I have a broad interest in data mining and knowledge discovery, marketing analytics, and business intelligence, with a particular interest in Business to Business (B2B) marketing customer behavior analysis. My current research develops efficient and effective techniques to address various B2B marketing challenges, to discover and understand the relationship among customer behavior and intention, and to ultimately assist intelligent business decision-making. My interests also extend to apply data mining techniques in other business domains, such as talent analytics, location intelligence, financial analysis.
